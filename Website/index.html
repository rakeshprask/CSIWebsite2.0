About

The Computer Society of India is the largest body of computer professionals in India. We are a group of skilled designers, developers and tech enthusiasts who engage in a lot of projects and hackathons. To help push technology forward, we organise a wide range of workshops, conferences, events and competitions both technical and non-technical.

Events

Devspace

Conference

Devspace 2018 was South India's largest student developers' conference with a footfall of 800 participants. Devspace included talks from Microsoft, Amazon and Nvidia followed by workshops and an expo with internship opportunities and on-spot competitions.
Visit devspace.tech for more information.

Codespace

Hackathon

Codespace is a competitive hackathon that has an over-arching central theme. Every year since 2015, Codespace has grown in scale and has been a huge success. Codespace 2018 was a 36-hour hackathon with prizes worth 15 lakhs in which over 100 teams competed on domains like Fintech, AR/VR, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing and Digital Literacy.
